Maximize Your ROI with Tax Credits

Partnering with Chroma isn't just an investment in your company culture—it's a smart financial move. State and Federal programs significantly offset the cost of providing childcare benefits.

Federal 45F Credit

Employer-Provided Child Care Credit

The IRS allows businesses to claim a tax credit of up to $150,000 annually (expanding significantly in 2026). This covers 25% of qualified childcare facility expenditures (such as contracting with Chroma for reserved spots) and 10% of resource and referral expenditures.

View IRS Form 8882

Georgia Employer's Credit

Georgia Child Care Tax Credit

Georgia offers one of the most generous incentives in the nation. Employers who purchase or sponsor childcare for employees can receive a tax credit equal to 75% of the employer's cost. This credit can be applied against 50% of your state income tax liability.

View Georgia DOR Details
