Prismpath™ Focus

Building Trust & Body.

In the first year, the brain grows faster than at any other time. Our infant curriculum is heavily weighted towards Physical development (motor skills, sensory processing) and Emotional security (attachment, regulation).

  • High Physical: Tummy time, rolling, reaching.
  • High Emotional: Responsive feeding, cuddling.
  • Emerging Social: Face recognition, babbling.

A Rhythm, Not a Routine

AM

Warm Welcome & Bottles

Transition from parent arms to teacher arms. Individual feeding schedules are strictly followed.

Mid

Sensory Discovery

Tummy time on textured mats, mirror play to build self-recognition, and soft music.

PM

Stroller Walks & Nap

Fresh air in our buggy carts (weather permitting) followed by restful sleep in dedicated cribs.
