Learning Journey

How learning evolves.

Our curriculum is not static. It shifts and matures alongside your child, moving from sensory-based discovery to logic-based inquiry.

Foundation (0-18 Months)

Focus on security and senses. Learning happens through touch, sound, and responsive caregiving.

Discovery (18 Months - 3 Years)

Focus on autonomy and language. "I can do it!" is the theme as we support potty training and early speech.

Readiness (3 Years - 5 Years)

Focus on executive function and logic. Multi-step projects, early writing, and complex social play prepare for Kindergarten.

Measuring Milestones

We don't just watch them grow; we measure it to ensure no child falls behind.

Developmental Screenings

We utilize the ASQ-3 (Ages & Stages Questionnaires) standard to conduct formal screenings at key age intervals. This helps us identify strengths and potential areas for early intervention support proactively.

  • Conducted at 4, 8, 12, 18, 24 Months
  • Partnership with Specialists

Formal Assessments

Twice a year (Fall and Spring), teachers conduct comprehensive assessments aligning with Georgia Early Learning and Development Standards (GELDS). These form the basis for our detailed Parent-Teacher Conferences.

  • Biannual Conferences
  • Individualized Lesson Planning
